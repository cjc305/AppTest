### Full description
```
Stuck trying to hit the 12-testers-for-14-days mark before Play Store launch?
You're not alone — it's the single biggest bottleneck for indie Android devs.

AppTest fixes it with reciprocity.

─── HOW IT WORKS ────────────────────────────────

1. POST YOUR APP
   Add your package name, Play Console opt-in URL, and how many testers you need.
   Your first app is free.

2. GET MATCHED — AUTOMATICALLY
   Every day at 02:00 UTC, AppTest's matching engine pairs developers who can
   genuinely help each other. Factors: app category, language, reputation tier,
   completion history, and available capacity.

3. TEST & EARN
   Install the matched app, run it daily, and stay in the test for 14 days.
   Your counterpart does the same for yours. Both sides earn reputation points
   and proof cards they can show to future testers or collaborators.

─── WHY IT WORKS ────────────────────────────────

✓ Real reciprocity — everyone is both dev and tester. No freeloaders.
✓ Reputation system — your completion rate, streak, and volume build a
  permanent score (Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum). Higher tier = better
  matches = faster closed-test completion.
✓ Proof cards — each completed test generates a signed card showing the app,
  dates, and your reputation delta. Screenshot it for your Play Console.
✓ Anti-cheat — emulators, Xposed hooks, and multi-accounting are detected
  and result in permanent bans. Your testers are real.

─── GROUND RULES ────────────────────────────────

• No cash payments, no fake reviews, no "I'll subscribe to your channel" deals.
• Every tester is a real Android developer with skin in the game.
• Abandoning a test hurts your reputation score — permanently.

─── WHO IT'S FOR ────────────────────────────────

Individual and small-team Android developers preparing their first (or next)
Play Store launch. If you have an app that needs 12 real testers, you're in
the right place.

─── WHAT'S NEXT ─────────────────────────────────

V2: AI-powered matchmaking based on user embeddings + bi-directional tester
ratings. V3: team testing pools, subscription plans, AI beta analysis reports.

→ Join the network. Hit your 12/14. Ship.
```

### Full description
```
卡在「需要 12 位 tester 測試 14 天才能上架 Play Store」這一關？
你不孤單——這是所有獨立 Android 開發者最常碰到的瓶頸。

AppTest 用互惠解決這個問題。

─── 運作方式 ─────────────────────────────────────

1. 登錄你的 App
   貼上 package name、Play Console 加入測試連結，設定需要的 tester 數量。
   第一個 App 完全免費。

2. 自動配對
   每天 02:00 UTC，配對引擎會根據 App 類別、語言、信譽等級、完成紀錄
   和空餘配額，自動把彼此能幫助的開發者配在一起。

3. 測試 + 累積信譽
   安裝對方的 App、每天開啟、堅持 14 天。
   對方也為你做同樣的事。雙方都獲得信譽分和可分享的完成證明卡。

─── 為什麼有效 ───────────────────────────────────

✓ 真正的互惠 — 每個人同時是開發者也是測試者，沒有白搭便車的空間。
✓ 信譽系統 — 完成率、連勝數和測試量累積成永久分數：
  銅牌 → 銀牌 → 金牌 → 白金。等級越高，配對越好，封閉測試完成越快。
✓ 完成證明卡 — 每次完成測試都會生成一張含 App 資訊、測試日期、
  信譽變化的簽名卡，可截圖附在 Play Console 中。
✓ 反作弊 — 模擬器、Xposed 框架、多帳號行為會被偵測，永久封禁。
  你收到的 tester 都是真人。

─── 基本規則 ─────────────────────────────────────

• 禁止金錢交易、假評論、互訂頻道等不正當交換。
• 每位 tester 都是有真實 App 在跑的 Android 開發者。
• 中途放棄測試會永久影響信譽分數。

─── 適合誰 ───────────────────────────────────────

準備第一次（或下一次）上架 Play Store 的個人或小團隊 Android 開發者。
如果你有一個需要 12 位真實 tester 的 App，這裡就是你該來的地方。

─── 接下來 ───────────────────────────────────────

V2：基於用戶向量的 AI 配對 + 雙向 tester 評分。
V3：團隊測試池、訂閱方案、AI 測試分析報告。

→ 加入網路。湊滿 12/14。上架。
```
